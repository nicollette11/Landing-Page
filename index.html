The Impeachment of President Donald Trump In early 2020, the United States witnessed the impeachment trial of President Donald Trump on charges of abuse of power and obstruction of Congress. Though acquitted by the Senate, the event marked only the third time in U.S. history that a president faced impeachment, highlighting deep divisions within the country.

Throwback:

Photo by Sebastian Ervi on https://www.pexels.com/photo/people-in-concert-1763075/
Music in the Early 2000s and '90s The late '90s and early 2000s were a golden era for music, spanning a range of genres from grunge to hip-hop to pop. Artists like Britney Spears, NSYNC, and Eminem dominated the charts and airwaves. The decade also gave rise to iconic bands like Nirvana and Pearl Jam, whose influence can still be felt today. These periods were marked by the rise of MTV and the importance of music videos, setting the stage for the digital age of music that would follow.
